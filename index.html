Bluetooth Mic Switch

Fix macOS audio issues by auto-switching to your internal mic when Bluetooth connects

Simple. Automatic. Reliable.

Tired of terrible Bluetooth microphone quality on your Mac? This app automatically detects when your Bluetooth headphones connect and instantly switches the audio input to your Mac's built-in microphone. Enjoy clear audio without manual adjustments!

Download for macOS

Key Features:

  • Auto-detects Bluetooth device connections
  • Instantly switches input to internal mic
  • Set-and-forget background operation
  • No complex setup needed
  • Supports AirPods, Beats, Sony, Bose, and more
  • Launch at login option
